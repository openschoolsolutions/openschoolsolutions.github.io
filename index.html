A teacher should be a coach who prepares students for life after school Why

We believe that students should foster their creativity and curiosity within the classroom. The teacher must support the learning of the student, defining meaningful topics to study and measuring the knowledge being applied to real life.

Our concept Concept

We enable your school to implement the progressive OPENSchool method without additional work for teachers, so that they can focus all their efforts on supporting their students. Our platform supports teachers so they can create individual learning paths for every student according to their needs, interests and plans for the future.

Benefits Benefits

Teachers

Have learning information about each individual student at your fingertips. Have an easy method to assign individual exercises to students.

Students

Get learning paths to master competences with the optimal use of time and effort. Easily keep track of your learning outcomes.

Schools

Become an innovation leader. Push the change for a better school system. Get access to a network of schools looking for a change.
