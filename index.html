A teacher should be a coach who prepares students for life after school Our values

We believe that students should foster their creativity and curiosity within the classroom. The teacher must support the learning of the student, defining meaningful topics to study and measuring the knowledge being applied to real life.

Our concept Concept

We enable your school to implement the progressive OPENSchool method without additional work for teachers, so that they can focus all their efforts on supporting their students. Our platform helps teachers create individual learning paths for every student according to their needs, interests and plans for the future.
